Welcome

A Construal is a kind of animated story. It may be a story about a phenomenon (e.g. the solar system), a problem (e.g. penny rolling), or just an everyday experience (e.g. using a lift, or shopping), or many other kinds of thing - a game, a puzzle, something in history or a complicated theory. Unless it's a very simple story (e.g. the Dog construal, or Whack-a-Mole) it will be split up into sections.
