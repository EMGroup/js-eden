To load the construal, follow these steps:

1. Go to http://jseden.dcs.warwick.ac.uk/master.jbnew/

2. Cut and paste the contents of the files 

preamblejbnew.js-e and additionalfuncs.js-e

into the Input Window and press Submit.

3. Select JSPE from the Project List panel (this will introduce a panel that obscure the Input Window).

4. Cut and paste the contents of the file commentary.js-e into the Input Window and press Submit

5, Follow the instructions for loadng the files  musicapp1.js-e and piano.js-e given on the sec ond slide.

The sequence of slides that follows illustrates some of the principles of making construals, and goes some way
towards making a working prototype.
